## ğŸ¯ Project: Product Classification

### Problem Statement
Classify e-commerce products into their correct categories based on product descriptions, titles, and metadata.

### Approach
1. **Text Preprocessing:** Tokenization, lemmatization, stopword removal
2. **Feature Extraction:** TF-IDF, Word2Vec embeddings
3. **Model Training:** Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost
4. **Ensemble Methods:** Voting classifier, stacking
5. **Evaluation:** F1-score, confusion matrix analysis
